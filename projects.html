Personal Robot

I'm currently in the process of designing and building a personal robot. This project involves learning about electronics, programming, and mechanical engineering. The goal is to create a robot that can assist with everyday tasks and serve as a learning platform for future robotics projects.

So far, I've completed the initial design phase and have started prototyping. I'm using open-source hardware and software to keep costs down and make the project accessible to others interested in robotics.

Financial Strategy

I'm currently reading books on personal finance and investing, and I've started a small business to gain practical experience. This project is teaching me valuable lessons about money management, risk assessment, and the importance of long-term planning.

Academic Excellence

Maintaining excellent grades is another key project. I'm involved in various school activities, including the robotics club and science fairs, where I can apply what I've learned and collaborate with like-minded peers.
